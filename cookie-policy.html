Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2024

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us make your experience better by remembering your preferences and providing essential functionality.

How We Use Cookies

Food Delight uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Authentication cookies: Keep you signed in to your account
  • Performance cookies: Improve website speed and performance

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often. This data helps us optimize the website and make it easier to navigate.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language or the region you are in) and provide enhanced features.

4. Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience of the site. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your browser
  • Set your browser to prevent cookies from being set
  • Accept or decline cookies when you first visit our site

Third-Party Cookies

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third party cookies you might encounter through this site:

  • Google Analytics cookies help us understand how you use the site
  • Social media cookies enable sharing functionality
  • Payment processor cookies for secure transactions

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. When we do, we will revise the 'last updated' date at the top of this policy.
